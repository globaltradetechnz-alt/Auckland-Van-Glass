Windscreen Insurance Claims in New Zealand

Most van windscreen, side and rear glass jobs are covered by insurance. We deal with all major NZ insurers and handle the paperwork so you don't have to.

How to File a Van Windscreen Insurance Claim

Filing a windscreen claim for your van is usually straightforward. Most comprehensive policies in New Zealand cover windscreen, side and rear glass replacement. Here's how the process works from start to finish.

1

Check Your Policy and Excess

Log into your insurer's app or portal and check whether windscreen glass is covered on your policy. Most comprehensive policies include it, but some require a separate glass add-on. Note your excess amount — it often ranges from $0 to $200 for windscreen claims, though some insurers offer zero-excess windscreen cover.

2

Contact Your Insurer or Us First

You can call your insurer directly or call us on 09 972-1091 first. We deal with all major NZ insurers and can help you start the claim, explain what glass your van needs, and give you a time estimate. Many insurers allow us to handle the claim process on your behalf once you give them permission.

3

Get Your Van Details Ready

Have your registration number, van make/model/year, and a description of the damage ready. Take clear photos of the damaged glass from inside and outside the van. If the glass is unsafe to drive — for example, a shattered windscreen or missing side glass — let your insurer know immediately. They may authorise urgent replacement.

4

Book the Replacement and We Invoice Your Insurer

We source the correct glass for your van, book a mobile fitting at your location, and invoice your insurance company directly. You may need to pay your excess to us or your insurer depending on the policy. Most jobs are done same-day or next-day across Auckland.

No problem. We offer competitive pricing for private van glass replacement. We can give you a firm price once we know your van's make, model and year. Most van windscreen replacements range from $250 to $500 depending on the glass and features. Get in touch for a price.
