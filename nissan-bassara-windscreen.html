Nissan Bassara Windscreen Replacement

Cracked or chipped windscreen on your Nissan Bassara? We replace Nissan Bassara windscreens and glass across Auckland - the JU30 generation - mobile to your home, depot or job site, same day where possible, with your insurance claim handled.

Nissan Bassara glass we replace

As well as windscreens, we replace Nissan Bassara sliding-door and side glass, quarter glass and rear / tailgate glass, including heated rear screens and wiper-fitted units. All glass is OEM-quality and bonded to manufacturer specification.
