About Auckland Van Glass

We're Auckland-based van glass specialists. Our focus is simple: give van owners and fleet operators honest advice, quality workmanship, and a mobile service that fits around the way they work.

Why We Do Things Differently

When most people search for "windscreen repair", they're actually dealing with damage that needs full replacement. We've seen too many van owners get a chip repair that should have been a replacement — leaving them back at square one a week later.

That's why we don't offer chip or crack repair. We focus on reinstating your van to its original, pre-damage state: full glass replacement, done properly, with quality glass and professional fitting. If you've got a small repairable chip, we'll point you to the chip-repair specialists on 0800 202 888 — no wasted time for you or us.

We specialise in vans because vans are different. Bigger, more curved windscreens, bonded side and rear glass, rain sensors and driver-assist cameras, and owners who can't afford long downtime. We're set up for exactly that.

And we're mobile — we come to you. No waiting rooms, no dropping the van off. We work around your schedule, whether that's your depot, a job site, your home or your office, anywhere across Auckland.
