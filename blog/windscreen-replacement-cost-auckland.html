Windscreen Replacement Cost in Auckland

What does a van windscreen replacement actually cost in Auckland? We give you the honest numbers and explain what drives the price up or down.

Typical Price Range: $250 – $500

For most vans in Auckland, a full windscreen replacement costs between $250 and $500 including the glass, fitting, and mobile service. This is the consensus range across the industry for standard van windscreens fitted at your location.

Your actual price may vary depending on your van's make, model, year, and the features built into the glass. Here's what affects the final cost.

What Affects the Price?

  • Van make and model — Common vans like the Toyota Hiace and Ford Transit have widely available glass, which keeps costs down. Rare imports, older models, or specialist vans may need glass sourced from overseas, increasing the price.
  • Glass features — Rain sensors, ADAS cameras, acoustic interlayers, heated elements, and tinted glass all add to the cost. A basic windscreen is cheaper than one with multiple built-in features.
  • Side and rear glass — Side window replacement typically ranges from $180 to $350. Rear glass for vans with barn doors or tailgates can be $200 to $400 depending on size and whether it includes heating elements.
  • Mobile fitting — We include mobile fitting in our pricing. Some workshops charge extra for mobile service; we don't — it's standard.
  • Insurance claims — If you're claiming through insurance, you usually only pay your excess (often $0 to $200). We invoice the insurer directly for the rest.

How to Get a Firm Price

The fastest way to get a firm price is to call us on 09 972-1091 with your van's make, model, year, and registration number. We can check glass availability and give you a price on the spot. Alternatively, send your details through our Help Form and we'll be in touch quickly.

Why We Don't Do Chip Repairs

We only do full glass replacement. If you have a small, repairable chip, you're better off calling the chip-repair specialists on 0800 202 888. They handle chip repairs cheaply — we handle full replacements done properly.
